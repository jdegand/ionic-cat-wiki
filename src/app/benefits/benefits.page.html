Reported Health Benefits of Cats

Owning a cat is reported to have many health benefits including:

Lower Stress and Anxiety
Playing with a cat can make you forget about your troubles. A cat can provide many laughs, as you can see from all the funny videos online.
 Prevent allergies
Exposing children to a cat can help strengthen their immune system.
 Reduce feelings of loneliness
Cats can be great companions and give you a sense of purpose.
 Can you really believe it?
A lot of these scientific studies probably have tons of flaws. Selection bias is a big issue in pet ownership studies. Are cat owners already happy and healthy? If you choose to own a pet, you are likely financially secure, which contributes to improved health. Surveys are used to measure pet ownership satisfaction. People may feel guilty about admitting it was a mistake to get a pet. I believe that many of these studies are likely conducted by the pet owners themselves in an effort to support their decision. Despite the happiness that comes with owning a pet, there is always pain in the event of its death. Don't think owning a pet will magically fix your problems. After a while, your happiness will regress to your mean happiness.
